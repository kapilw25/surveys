\subsection{Sim-to-Real and Cross-Embodiment Transfer}
\label{sec:transfer}

A skill is only useful if it moves across robots. Cross-embodiment datasets and shared
action spaces train one policy for many bodies~\citep{openx}; UniStore's ``one skill,
G1/H1/B2/Go2'' claim (\S\ref{sec:open}) is this problem in production.
